Challenge Pack

A Card Deck to Elevate Your Creative Thinking Game

Originating from the disparity between academic literature and industry application on the topic of creativity enhancement, the Challenge Pack was created to help bridge this gap and provide an opportunity for those outside academia to build their creativity and creative thinking skills.

The card deck has a variety of activities classified under two different categories: Design Challenges and Brainteasers. In order to quickly determine the activity type, the cards are outlined in different colors. Light green is for a design challenge, blue is for a brainteaser, and gray is for information cards such as answer and creativity concept keys. If you would like to learn about other design decisions, development process, or academic literature in which the Challenge Pack is based upon, feel free to check out the About This Project tab above.

When to Use the Challenge Pack

The Challenge Pack is a great tool to use...

  • As an ice breaker at the beginning of a meeting to get everyone's brains thinking
  • As a break from work tasks
  • When you're fixated and/or frustrated with a task and need to reset
  • As a team building tool for teammates, work colleagues, or students
  • As weekly challenges to build comradery
  • To introduce engineering and ideation to students

How to Use the Challenge Pack

1. Review the card symbols and creativity key.
2. Scroll through the card deck and pick a card.
3. Read card to learn activity instructions, materials and number of participants needed for each activity, and the creativity concepts utilized from academic literature.
